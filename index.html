Project Information

IoT-Based Flood Monitoring And Alert System

Initiated under the Brgy Mamatid City of Cabuyao Laguna, this localized IoT flood alert system serves as Barangay Mamatid's structural early warning and monitoring protocol.

This platform coordinates with live sensor which directly from specialized ultrasonic sensors positioned across critical river channels. Data is refreshed continuously to deliver proactive situational awareness to communities before severe water level changes occur.
